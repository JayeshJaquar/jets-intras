How We Use Cookies?

Our Crypto-currency Dashboard uses cookies (and similar storage methods like localStorage) to:

  • Make the dashboard feel consistent every time you return

Essential Cookies These are required for basic functionality: Remembering your selected currency type Maintaining app state during usage Without these, some features may not work properly. 🔹 Preference Cookies These help personalize your experience: suggestions UI preferences They simply make the app feel more you.
